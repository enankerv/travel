Chuck a link in

Paste an Airbnb (or other listing) URL and we'll scrape it, summarize it, and build a report. Optionally set dates and guests so the crawler can see availability and pricing.

Can't scrape? Paste listing text

For sites we can't crawl (e.g. Airbnb), open the listing, Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C, and paste below. Images are grabbed automatically from the clipboard.
